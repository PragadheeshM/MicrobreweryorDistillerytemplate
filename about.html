The Beginning

Two Friends, One Dream

In the winter of 2010, childhood friends Marcus Chen and David O'Brien turned their garage homebrew hobby into something bigger. Armed with little more than a secondhand copper kettle and an obsessive love for craft beer, they founded Copper & Cask in Portland's Pearl District.

What started as weekend experiments quickly gained a cult following at local farmers markets. By 2013, they'd moved into a converted warehouse, added a small distillery, and never looked back. Today, Copper & Cask produces over 50 unique craft beers and 12 premium spirits, distributed across the Pacific Northwest.

Our Journey

Milestones That Matter

A decade of passion, craft, and community — one batch at a time.

2010 Milestone

The Garage Era

Founded by Marcus & David with a single copper kettle and a dream. First batch: 5 gallons of amber ale.

2013 Milestone

The Warehouse

Moved into a 5,000 sq ft converted warehouse. Added distillery operations and opened our first public taproom.

2017 Milestone

National Recognition

Won "Best New Brewery" at the Great American Beer Festival and expanded distribution to 5 states.

2024 Milestone

The Expansion

Opened a second location, launched online ordering, and introduced our signature spirits line nationwide.
