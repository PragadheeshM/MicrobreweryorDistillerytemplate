Our Heritage

A Legacy Brewed in Tradition

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Portland, Copper & Cask started as a passion project between two friends who believed that great beer and spirits should tell a story.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art facility with hand-built copper kettles, producing over 50 unique craft beers and a growing lineup of small-batch spirits — all sustainably sourced and locally crafted.

How We Craft

From Grain to Glass

Every sip tells a story. Here's the journey behind every Copper & Cask creation.

01

Select

Hand-picking the finest hops, grains, and botanicals from trusted local farms.

02

Brew

Mashing, boiling, and fermenting in our hand-built copper kettles with artisan precision.

03

Age

Patient barrel aging in charred American oak for depth, character, and complexity.

04

Pour

Served fresh at our taproom or delivered to your door — crafted perfection in every glass.
