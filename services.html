What We Offer

From grain to glass — explore the complete Copper & Cask experience.

Craft Beers

Over 50 unique recipes — from citrusy IPAs and smooth stouts to refreshing wheat ales and seasonal specials. Each beer is brewed in copper kettles using organic ingredients.

  • Indian Pale Ales (IPA)
  • Stouts & Porters
  • Wheat & Sour Ales
  • Seasonal & Limited Releases

Small Batch Spirits

Premium whiskey, gin, and vodka distilled with precision in our copper pot stills. Aged in charred American oak barrels for depth and character.

  • Single Malt Whiskey
  • Botanical Dry Gin
  • Copper-Pot Vodka
  • Barrel-Aged Rum

Brewery Tours

Step behind the scenes and witness our entire process — from mashing and fermenting to distilling and bottling. Includes a guided tasting flight.

  • 90-Minute Guided Tour
  • Tasting Flight Included
  • Group Discounts Available
  • Weekends at 2pm & 5pm
